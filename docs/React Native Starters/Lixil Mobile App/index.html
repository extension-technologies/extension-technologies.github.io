Attendance Page#

  • To mark attendance on daily basis click on attendance from bottom navigation menu and your attendance page will open as shown.
  • Date and Time are auto fetched.
  • Then click on Enter to enter the attendance.
  • Click on submit to submit the attendance.

When You click on Enter, It consists of four types â€“

  • Present
  • Absent
  • On Leave
  • Half Day

Fill the details as per your plan and click on Ok and then click on SUBMIT to successfully mark your attendance.

Open Ticket#

  • Click on Open Calls to see the open tickets.
  • Click on Closed Calls to see closed calls
  • Click on Pending Calls to see pending calls
  • Click on Escalation to see escalated calls

You can see all your open tickets here.

Ticket Info#

When you open a ticket you will see ticket status and its related information in these 4 tabs.

  • Customer
  • Call
  • Product
  • Defect Details

Click on Call to see the call information.

Click on Customer to see the Customer Information.

Connect With Customer#

Click on connect to connect with customer. There are three options to connect with customers.

  • Call
  • SMS
  • Whatsapp

When you click on call, you will redirected to dialer of your phone, and your ticket status changed to Responded back.

Click on check in button during your visit and your ticket status get changed to Repair Started.

Click on product and then click on edit to add the products details.

There are two options to search the model.

  • BY NAME
  • BY NO.

Click on the model and and all the details get auto filled. Click on Submit to submit the details

Product Information made for that particular ticket will also get fetched from mobile application to Zoho CRM and will appear here inside Product Information..

Click on Check Out button to make check out.

Fill the Distance and click on Ok.

Repair Status#

There are Two options

  • Repair Done- If repair is done.
  • Pending- If some part is needed to fix the product.

Click on any one option.

If repair is done.Then Other Page Open Up

All the Repair Information made for that particular ticket will also get fetched from mobile application to Zoho CRM and will shown here in Repair Information section.

Click on anyone to fill the status
